GLIMPSES OF PROPERTY

Featuring luxurious rooms with views of a garden or the surrounding mountains, Wildflower Hall Shimla features heated indoor and outdoor pools.
Other recreation facilities include the Oberoi Spa and a fitness center. Free WiFi is available in the rooms of the property.

The air-conditioned rooms feature large bay windows offering garden or mountain views, a satellite TV, DVD player and mini-bar.
A separate walk-in closet has an electronic safe. 24-hour butler service is available.

Wildflower Hall Shimla is 8.1 mi from the city of Shimla, which is just next to Charabra Village. It takes approximately a 1.5-hour drive to reach Shimla Airport.
